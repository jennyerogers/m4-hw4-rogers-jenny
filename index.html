Frank Ocean

A brief overview of my favorite artist:

About

Frank Ocean is an American singer and songwriter from New Orleans, Louisiana. Winner of two Grammy awards, Ocean has left his mark on the music industry.

I first discovered Frank Ocean in middle school. The first song I ever listened to was "Thinkin Bout You." I loved the rawness of his lyrics paired with his distinct, smooth voice. Ever since, I've been a huge fan!

My favorite song by Frank Ocean is called "Self Control," off his "Blonde" album.

Though he is well-loved, Ocean is notorious for hardly showing face in public or giving performances. He has not released a studio album since his 2016 album titled "Blonde."

Fans have been awaiting another release of a studio album for years, but Ocean has made no comment on when the next release will be. Fans are hopeful that Ocean will drop new music before his 2023 Coachella performance.

Discography

Frank Ocean only has two studio albums, along with a handful of singles. His first album, titled "channel ORANGE" released in 2012, featuring popular songs like "Thinkin Bout You," "Lost," and "Pyramids."

His second studio album, titled "Blonde," features fan-favorites like "Nights," "Pink + White," and "Ivy."

Upcoming Performances

After a lengthy hiatus, Frank Ocean is set to headline Coachella 2023. He was originally on the 2020 lineup before COVID-19 cancelled the festival. Thousands of festival-goers are anticipating Ocean's return!
